Privacy Policy

Checkpoint · Last updated July 2026

The short version: Checkpoint stores all of your data on your own device. Nothing is collected, transmitted, sold, or shared. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party services.

Data storage

All budgeting data you enter — income, expenses, categories, subscriptions, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device using your browser or app's local storage. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

Data collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically, the app has:

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that receive your data
  • No network transmission of your financial information

Backups and exports

The app includes manual export features (JSON backup and CSV). Files you export are saved where you choose and are entirely under your control.

Data deletion

You can erase all app data at any time via Settings → Reset all data, or by uninstalling the app. Because we hold no copies, deletion on your device is complete and permanent.

Children

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy
